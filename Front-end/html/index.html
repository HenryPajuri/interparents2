European Schools Parent Association

INTERPARENTS is the umbrella association for the Parent Associations of the European Schools, bringing together parents from across Europe to support quality education and advocate for student welfare.

Learn More About Us

Introduction

INTERPARENTS serves as the central coordinating body for Parent Associations across all European Schools. We work tirelessly to ensure that the voices of parents are heard in educational policy decisions and that the highest standards of education are maintained across the European Schools system.

Our organization facilitates communication between parent associations, provides support and guidance on educational matters, and represents parent interests in various European Schools committees and working groups.
